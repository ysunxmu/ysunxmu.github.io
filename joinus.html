We always welcome self-motivated students to join us! The student's research and academic background might be in one or more of the following areas: physics, chemistry, materials science and engineering. Experience with ab initio calculations and molecular dynamics is highly valued. Undergraduate and Master's students are encouraged to contact Prof. Yang Sun to express interest in pursuing a PhD degree.

We have long been providing research opportunities for undergraduate students and welcome those interested in physics and programming to join our team (with no restrictions on grade level). We offer support for various practical opportunities, including bachelor's thesis research design and research assistant positions.

课题组长期招收优秀硕士研究生、博士研究生以及博士后，欢迎青年才俊加入。申请条件：

  1. 物理、化学、材料背景，具备良好的专业知识和科研能力，有第一性原理计算和分子动力学模拟经验的优先；
  2. 具有良好的中英文写作和交流能力；
  3. 热爱科研、勤奋踏实、诚实守信，有团队协作精神。

长期为本科生提供科研条件，欢迎对物理和编程感兴趣的同学加入团队(对年级无限定)。可支持申报大学生创新创业项目、本科毕业设计以及科研助理等多种实践机会。
