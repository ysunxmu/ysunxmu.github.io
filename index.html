About us

We develop theoretical methods and algorithms for atomistic simulations of the structure and phase transitions of matter under extreme conditions. These techniques allow us to study problems ranging from the electronic structure and materials properties at the microscopic scale to the formation and evolution of planetary interiors.

Join Us

We welcome motivated students and postdocs with backgrounds in physics, chemistry, and materials science to join us at the Department of Physics, Xiamen University, located on beautiful Xiamen Island on the southeastern coast of China.
